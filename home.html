Make things, solve problems

Adam is a tech guy, occasional robotics engineer, company founder, and dabbles in the arts.

Adam's best job title is something like 0-1 Product Engineer - he brings things into existence through sheer willpower and technical literacy. This makes him very good at high-performance consulting where you have to make a *thing* that is going to solve some business problem. He used to do that all the time but now spends half his time building automated businesses and the other half helping others transform theirs.

He's obsessive and easily sidetracked by things that are beautiful, adventurous or solve little problems in his own life. This makes him less good at corporate life but means he gets up to some pretty cool stuff.
